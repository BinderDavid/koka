Operations on 'fixed'ed point numbers.
These have infinite range and precision for the whole part, and a precision of up to 15 digits after the dot. Internally they are represented by a infinite range intstd/core/int: V for the whole part and a doublestd/core/double: V to represent the fraction. A fixedstd/num/fixed/fixed: V generally has better performance than arbitrary precision decimals.

type fixedstd/num/fixed/fixed: V

A fixedstd/num/fixed/fixed: V has infinite range and a precision of up to 15 digits after the dot. Internally they are represented by a infinite range intstd/core/int: V for the whole part and a doublestd/core/double: V to represent the fraction. A fixedstd/num/fixed/fixed: V generally has better performance than arbitrary precision decimals.
